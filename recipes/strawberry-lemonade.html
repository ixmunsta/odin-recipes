Strawberry Lemonade

Make your own strawberry lemonade slushie

Ingredients

  • 2 cups ice cubes
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 (6 ounce) can frozen lemonade concentrate
  • 5 large fresh strawberries, hulled

Steps

  1. Place the ice cubes, water and lemonade concentrate into the container of a blender. Process until ice is finely blended. Add strawberries, and puree until well blended. Taste, and adjust flavors if you like.
